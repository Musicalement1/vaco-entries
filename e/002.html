Entry 002: Eye Walls

Eye Walls

Type: Anomaly

Category: C (Abomination)

Rarity: Extremely Common

Data/kit: 5

Hazard Index: 0 (Even more passive than the passiviest passive passant.)

These creatures have hard bodies that nothing known to man can break, they are docile, immobile creatures that cannot harm a soul, and it is theorized that a higher being utilizes these entities as cameras to see all who enter the Shattered Beyond.
