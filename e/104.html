Minor Void Mass (T1)

Type: Living Void Entity

Category: EF

Rarity: Uncommon

Hazard Index: 0.6 (it’s not killing you on it’s own)

A Fragment of void that came to life for an unknown reason. the mass of Minor Void Masses is significant enough for them to be an actual threat.
